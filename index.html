OUR PROCESS

HOW WE DO IT

One of our primary goals at PPG is to provide turn-key hardware and software solutions for your next project. Our tested and effective processes are the key to producing efficient and high quality results. Our unique experience with complete hardware and software integration allows us to provide you with the most robust and versatile set of tools to bring your idea to life.

01. RESEARCH

Effective and thorough team research ensures maximum project efficiency and product accuracy.

02. SKETCH

Proper planning across all facets of the project establishes high standards for quality from start to end.

03. REVIEW

Organized and scheduled scrums are critical to setting expectations and realistic milestone dates.

04. PROTOTYPE

Starting simple and strategically increasing depth is important to us. We ship early and often.

05. TEST

We believe in testing from day one. Ongoing feedback in all areas of a project is fundamental to success.

06. MANUFACTURE

It’s a big step getting from a product that works to one that works every time for years. We can help.
